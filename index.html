Shaikh Shawon Arefin Shimon

Lecturer, Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering (ECE), North South University

I am a Lecturer of Computer Science in the Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering (ECE), School of Engineering & Physical Sciences (SEPS) at North South University (NSU), Dhaka, Bangladesh. I am also working as the faculty coordinator for Career Development and Industrial Outreach Program for NSU School of Engienering & Physical Sciences. I received my Masters of Science in Computer Science from Colorado State University in 2016 where I worked under the supervision of Dr. Jaime Ruiz at Ruiz HCI Lab. I also hold a B.Sc in Computer Science & Engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering & Technology.
